Required Work

Each group must make a presentation summarizing the paper assigned to them (see table below). The presentation must cover the following aspects:

A clear description of the problem the paper is addressing; 
The solution proposed by the authors; 
The approach used to assess the effectiveness of the solution; 
The results of the evaluation; 
What are the pros and cons of using this technique? 
When would you recommend using the proposed visualization technique? 
What did you like or dislike about the paper? What would you change to improve it? 
Any other aspect of the paper you deem interesting.
Presentation Format 

Upload your presentation file  (PPT, PPTX, or PDF) before the session. 
10 minutes of presentation in-person + 5 minutes of Questions/Answers for each group. 
All members in each group must speak during the presentation.
Everyone must attend all presentations.
